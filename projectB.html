Kootour Golf
I-Design inside the Browser.

Kootour is a web application which the golf enthuistics can book teetime to play golf as well as booking golf packages which the players can play and enjoy the scenery of different popular golf courses. Typography

One of the challenge for me working on the product is picking a right Chinese font as I am not familiar with the Chinese language. After doing research on Chinese font and the discussion with the product owner, I learnt that there are two standard version: Simplifed Versionwhich are used in mainland China since 1949 and Tradtional Version a much more complex version which is still used in Taiwan. Like English fonts classification of San-serif and Serif fonts, there are two classifications in Chinese fonts: Songti & Heiti (a more modern font). Since Kootour targets mostly mainland and oversea Chinese, I chose Microsoft YaHei which also match the minimalist design appraoch of the product.
