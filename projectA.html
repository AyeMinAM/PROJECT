Travellers' Journey

A user who lands to the kootour website will see a tour and activity on a carefully design card yet to be easily implemented for the front end developer. I have already got Bootstrap’s card component while I am designing the card. If the pontential traveller is interested in the tour by simply looking at the main five features of the tour: price, image, name of the tour & activites, the name of the guide and a few description of the tour.

If the traveller is interested in the tour, she will click on the card which will bring her to the tour detail pages, where she can find detail information about the trip. The designer make use of the Bootstrap’s powerful 12 columns to make it responsive while keeping in mind about the most important information stay above the fold when viewed on mobile screen. (remain on the screen before it is scrolled down.

Once the traveller is interested in the tour, and when she books, she can either register if she is a new coustomer or she can log in to proceed the booking process. There is also “Book as a Guest” function for the users to skip these steps and make the booking process easier and accessible for those who do not want to signup.

Icons

Mono colour icons (either white or turquoise) are used at the pages where they are a lot of contents and pictures while icons with different colors are used at the pages where they are not lots of contents. Icons can be both with or without background. The background of the icon is square with rounded corners.

Typeface

Montserrat a open sources fonts created by and it can be downloaded on Google Fonts. There are nine different font style: Thin, Ultra-Light, Light, Regular, Medium, Semi-Bold, Bold, Extra-Bold and Black.

Kootour uses Light, Regular and Medium. The regular text is used ‘Light’ and header, title and text on the buttons are used Medium. Montserrat Regular font is too bold to use regular text and paragraph to match Kootour’s Design. Montserrat does not come with Italic font family,
